Welcome to GooFans Offline. We are a community website for World of Goo fans: a place to download new levels, mods and tools, and a resource to support those developing new ones. With a lively forum and an active community developing addins, the World of Goo just keeps on expanding. We're all in it together!

Rules Of GooFans Online/Offline, GooBroad & Important legal Rules in some countries/regions

  • GooFans Online/Offline Rules:
  • 1. Be respectful to every World of Goo player and developer.
  • 2. Do not upload stolen mods or fake projects.
  • 3. No spam, malicious files, or harmful links.
  • 4. Always credit original creators of levels and mods.
  • 5. Keep GooBroad discussions friendly and constructive.
  • 6. NSFW or offensive content is not allowed.
  • 7. Do not impersonate GooFans or 2D Boy staff members.
  • 8. Support preservation of classic World of Goo community content.
  • 9. Use GooBroad responsibly and avoid misinformation.
  • 10. Have fun building, sharing, and exploring Goo creations together!
  •  
  • GooBroad Rules:
  • 1. If your addin involves NSFW content, that's not allowed on GooBoard.
  • 2. If your addin uses assets from someone else's mod or assets someone else made, please make sure you give credit and get permission from the creator.
  • 3. If your addin is a standard one that adds custom content to World of Goo, it must be in the form of a ''.goomod'' file. If you upload a modified World of Goo installation, that's redistribution, which is not allowed on GooBoard. Instead, make a goomod file that only includes your custom content and can be easily compiled in with GooTool.
  • 4. If your addin somehow includes hidden malware in the files, that is not allowed on GooBoard. Your addin should be in the form of a ''.goomod'' anyway, unless it's a fangame, utility, etc. All downloads of your file must be completely risk-free.
  • 5. If your addin's description includes hate speech, harassment, misinformation, or harmful links, that's not allowed on GooBoard. Please keep your description on topic and straight-forward. All it should include is information about your addin.
  • 6. If your addin changes the gameplay of original levels or gooballs, that's not allowed on GooBoard. This can allow players to cheat at original game levels. For example, you could make common goo detachable which allows players to collect much more gooballs in some levels. You could also make Beauty and the Tentacle have 2 beauties, for example. This is not tolerated. NOTE: You CAN make visual changes to levels, which sometimes involves XML editing. But you cannot change the gameplay of a ball or level, just its visuals in order to make a ball style or theme pack.
  •  
  • Important legal Rules in some countries/regions:
  • 1. The content of the Addin document prohibits ethnic discrimination, oppression, and separatism, specifically aimed at preventing the separation of Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, southern Tibet, Aksai Chin, Diaoyu Islands, and the South China Sea Islands. (Rule From P.R.C. Constitution)
  • 2. Promoting all forms of militarism, fascism, historical revisionism, hate speech, and other acts of terrorism and drug trafficking are prohibited. Unless you want to use these to create satirical content and promote positive values. (Rule From The relevant laws and regulations of most anti-fascist countries, etc.)
  •  
  • All of the above rules are important, so please remember to follow them, and don't forget to have fun!
